How to Use

  • Templates: Save frequent transactions (like coffee or groceries) to log them in one click.
  • Filter History: Click any Category Title to see only its expenses in the history window.
  • Reset View: Click the "History: [Month]" header to see all expenses again.
  • Reorder: Drag the grid handle on category cards to sort them how you like.
  • Sync: Use Export/Import to backup or move your data between devices.
